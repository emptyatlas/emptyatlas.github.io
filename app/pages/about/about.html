Our Story

Empty Atlas

“How have I not heard this band before now?” Famous first words of every Empty Atlas fan. For the past decade, the Mississippi melodic indie rock act has assembled a trove of uncommon songs about common ground, reflecting on loss and worry, hope and persistence.

Heavy concepts, to be sure, but coated in Empty Atlas’s unmistakable blend of dynamic rock, hooky melodies and thoughtful lyrics. It’s what has earned them a swath of international accolades, including finalist spots in the John Lennon Songwriting Contest and American Songwriter Song Contest. Empty Atlas is currently working on their third full-length album.

For fans of Manchester Orchestra, Thrice, Death Cab for Cutie and The Killers.
