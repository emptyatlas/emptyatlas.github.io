BIO

Mississippi is a state known for its storytellers, a term that certainly applies to Empty Atlas. Formed in Central Mississippi in 2014, the band has become known for their combination of powerful lyrics and gripping melodic indie-rock.

Singer-songwriter Micah Smith fronts the group, which released its debut full-length album, “Hestia,” in December 2016, shortly before guitarist Brennan Michael White, bassist Alex Ingram and drummer Robert Currie Hansford joined to cement the permanent lineup.

Empty Atlas released its latest single, “Short Fiction,” in August 2018, mixed by Tyler Spratt and Grammy nominee Mark Needham (The Killers, Fleetwood Mac). They are currently touring regionally while working on their sophomore full-length album.

SHOW HIGHLIGHTS

Empty Atlas has performed with acts such as The Bright Light Social Hour, Broadside, Hodera, Jared & the Mill, Secret Stuff, Sweet Crude and The Vegabonds, and at events such as Amped & Wired, Cotton District Arts Festival, Jackson Indie Music Week and Memphis Music & Heritage Fest.
